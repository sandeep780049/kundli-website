Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Kundli?

A Kundli, or birth chart, is an astrological chart created based on your birth date, time, and place, representing the positions of planets and stars at your birth moment.

How is my Zodiac sign determined?

Your Zodiac sign is based on the position of the Sun on the date you were born, defining your personality traits and tendencies.

Why do you ask for my birth place?

Your birth place affects the local time and astrological calculations, providing a more accurate and personalized Kundli.

Are these tips 100% accurate?

This website provides general astrological guidance and tips. For precise and personal readings, please consult a professional astrologer.

Can I use this Kundli for major life decisions?

Use this tool as a guide, but always consider consulting experts before making significant life choices.

Do you store my personal information?

No, we do not store or share your birth date or place. All data is processed temporarily to generate your Kundli and tips.

How often should I check my Kundli?

You can revisit anytime, especially after major life changes, to gain new insights and guidance.

Is astrology scientifically proven?

Astrology is a traditional belief system and not a science. Many people find it useful for self-reflection, but results vary individually.

Can I trust the future tips?

Our tips are general and for guidance only. For personalized advice, consulting an experienced astrologer is recommended.
