Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Kundli?

A Kundli, or birth chart, is an astrological chart created based on your birth date, time, and place, representing the positions of planets and stars.

How is my Zodiac sign determined?

Your Zodiac sign is determined by the position of the Sun on the date of your birth.

Why do you ask for my birth place?

Your birth place affects the local time and can influence the astrological calculations, providing more personalized insights.

Are these tips 100% accurate?

This website provides general astrological guidance and tips. For personalized and detailed readings, consult a professional astrologer.

About This Website & Privacy Policy

This website is designed to provide you with a simple, free tool to generate your basic Kundli and receive astrology-based future tips. We do not store or share your personal data. Your birth date and place are used only temporarily to generate your Kundli and tips, and no data is saved.
